Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why do I need to run as administrator?

A: Administrator privileges are required for low-level mouse input access via SendInput API. This is a Windows security requirement.

Q: Does this work with anti-cheat?

A: LeverageONE runs externally and does not inject into game processes. However, use at your own risk and check your game's terms of service.

Q: How do I activate recoil compensation?

A: Hold Right Mouse Button + Left Click to activate. Use Right Mouse Button + Mouse Button 4 for rapid fire mode.

Q: What are the hotkeys?

A: INSERT or P toggles master enable/disable. SCROLL LOCK switches between SMG/AR modes.
